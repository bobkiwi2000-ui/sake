Data Sovereignty

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

🔒 We collect minimal data. Your server content is yours alone.

1. Information We Collect

Sake Digital collects only what's necessary to provide Minecraft hosting:

  • Email address and Discord ID (for account access)
  • Billing information (processed by Stripe/PayPal — we never see your full card details)
  • Server IP addresses and usage logs (for security and debugging)

We do NOT collect or store your Minecraft account credentials, world files (unless you grant support access), or real-time player chat logs.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your information is used exclusively for:

  • Provisioning and managing your server
  • Billing and fraud prevention
  • Responding to support requests
  • Improving our infrastructure (anonymized analytics only)

3. Third-Party Services

We rely on trusted partners to deliver our service:

  • Vultr / Oracle Cloud — Infrastructure providers (servers are in Sydney, Singapore)
  • Stripe / PayPal — Payment processing (we never store card details)
  • Discord — Community and support

Each partner has its own privacy policy. We recommend reviewing them.

4. Data Retention

We keep your data while your account is active. After cancellation:

  • Server files: deleted after 7 days (you can download them first)
  • Account information: anonymized within 30 days
  • Billing records: retained for 5 years (Australian tax law)

5. Your Rights (Australian Privacy Act)

You have the right to:

  • Access all personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (except where required by law)

To exercise these rights, contact us via Discord.

6. Security

We use industry-standard encryption (TLS 1.3) for all data transmission. Our infrastructure partners maintain SOC2 compliance. However, no system is 100% secure — you use our service at your own risk.

7. Changes to This Policy

We'll notify you of material changes via email or Discord at least 14 days in advance. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

8. Contact

Privacy questions? Reach out on Discord.
