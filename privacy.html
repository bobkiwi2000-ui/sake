Data Sovereignty

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Information We Collect

Sake Digital collects core identification metrics necessary to provision server environments, including your username, email address, and billing coordinates processed via our third-party checkout gateways. We also retain community-specific information (such as Discord IDs) for routing priority tier allocations.

2. How Data Is Applied

Your operational metrics are leveraged exclusively to initialize instances, maintain network security, and handle billing protocols. We enforce strict data separation barriers—your server files, database contents, and memory configurations belong purely to you and are never crawled, indexed, or reviewed unless explicit operational support is initiated by your account.

3. Payment Infrastructure Security

Financial parameters are handled externally by premium global financial gateways (Stripe/PayPal). Sake Digital never caches or processes raw credit card values or structural banking coordinates on our primary routing matrices.

4. Infrastructure Logs & Retention

System metrics (such as console outputs, transient error logs, and connection timestamps) are held temporarily on node clusters to isolate hardware errors and optimize DDoS mitigation responses. Player telemetry collected via game instances remains subject to local retention parameters handled directly by the server operator.

5. Data Rectification Rights

In accordance with applicable privacy codes within Australia, users maintain full authority to inspect, update, or completely request the deletion of their personal administrative profile data. Purging billing identifiers may result in automatic environment teardowns due to loss of verifiable service accounts.
