If an instance becomes the target of sustained network assaults that threaten to degrade our broader core routing infrastructure, we reserve the right to temporarily null-route or isolate that specific target instance to shield our global network footprint.

To maintain storage integrity and capacity for active clients across our primary high-speed NVMe arrays, files and instance configurations associated with unpaid or inactive accounts will be permanently purged from our hardware arrays seven (7) days following initial service suspension. Regular external snapshot generation remains the user's operational responsibility.
